Getting Started

Welcome to the School Event Management System (SEMS). This platform centralizes all campus activities, allowing for seamless event planning, registration, and attendance tracking.

Registration Policy All new accounts require Admin Approval. You cannot log in immediately after signing up; you must wait for an administrator to activate your account (usually within 24-48 hours).
How to Register
  1. Click "Create Account" on the login page.
  2. Personal Info: Enter your First Name, Last Name, and valid Email.
  3. Academic Info: Select your Course (e.g., BSIT, BSBA) first, then select your specific Section from the dropdown.
  4. Credentials: Choose a unique Username and a password (min. 8 characters).
  5. Submit. You will see a green "Registration Successful" overlay.
Login Issues

If you cannot log in:

  • Pending Approval: Your account might still be under review by an admin.
  • Forgotten Password: Currently, password resets are handled manually by the IT Department or Student Council admins.

Account Types & Permissions

The system adapts based on your role. Select your role above to highlight relevant sections.

  • Students / Participants: Can browse calendar, register for events, and view their own attendance history.
  • Student Organization Officers: Have "Proposals" access to submit event requests for approval.
  • Staff / Faculty: Full event creation privileges, AI tool access, and ability to scan student QR codes.
  • Admins: Global control. Can approve users, approve events, and manage system configuration (training data).

Finding Events

Your dashboard is your central hub.

  • Event Calendar: A monthly view of all approved campus activities. Click any day to see details.
  • My Registrations: Tracks events you have joined. Includes your status (Registered vs. Waitlisted).
  • My Feedback: Submit ratings and comments for events you attended.

Registration & Waitlists

Events have capacity limits.

The Waitlist System If an event is full, joining adds you to the Waitlist. If a confirmed participant cancels, the system automatically promotes the next person on the waitlist. Always check your status before the event!

Attendance & QR Codes

We use a paperless QR system.

  1. Go to "My Registrations".
  2. Find the event and click "Show QR Code" (or "Download").
  3. Present this code at the venue.
  4. Once scanned, your status updates to "Present" immediately.

Creating an Event Proposal

The Event Form is divided into specialized tabs for detailed planning.

1. Details Tab
Basic info: Name, Type (Academic, Sports, etc.), Dates, and Venue. The system automatically checks for venue conflicts.
2. Equipment Tab
Request items from inventory (Projectors, Mics, Tables). Items are categorized (Audio/Visual, Furniture, Sports). The AI may highlight recommended items in green.
3. Smart Budget Tab
Define your budget breakdown. The AI Auto-Fill can estimate costs based on your attendee count.
4. Timeline Tab
Create a minute-by-minute schedule. Critical for logistics to know when to set up.

Using the AI Assistant

The "Smart AI Trainer" utilizes historical data to help you plan.

  • Predictive Budgeting: Suggests budget allocation accurately based on past similar events.
  • Resource Recommendations: If you select "Concert", it reminds you to book "Speakers" and "Lighting".

Managing Attendees

On event day, use the QR Scanner module:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Scan student phones. Success/Error messages appear instantly.
  3. Manual Check-in: If a student has no phone, type their Username or ID in the manual entry box.

Approvals & Moderation

Central control center for quality assurance.

  • User Approvals: Verify that new registrations match the selected Course/Section before activating.
  • Event Proposals: Review budget feasibility and venue availability. You can Approve, Reject (with reason), or return for Revision.
  • Conflict Resolution: The system flags double-booked venues in red. You have the authority to override conflicts if needed.

Analytics & Reporting

Data-driven insights.

  • Department Budget Table: Track spending per department (BSIT, BSBA, etc.).
  • Attendance Heatmaps: See which event types get the most engagement.
  • Training Data: You can curate the "AI Memory" by adding successful past events to the training set, making future predictions smarter.
